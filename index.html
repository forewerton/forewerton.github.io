VDI UX Benchmarking

User Research

I participated in a benchmarking to investigate the UX aspects of three VDI solutions (Virtual Desktop Infrastructure) with assistance of eye tracking data.

10 VDI specialists participated in the study answering questionnaires and performing similar tasks in the three evaluated tools. We analyzed both qualitative and quantitative data using objective metrics, such as, performance, and satisfaction, besides comments and the visual behavior of participants.

  • Client: LG

Accessibility in Smartphones

User Research

I conducted 2 workshops with groups of visually impaired people to understand how they use and make sense of current mobile technologies. This study served as input to conceive an accessibility framework for touchscreen smartphones.

  • Client: CPqD Alcance App

Smart Grid Field Study

User Research

I participated in the preparation and application of questionnaires, and in field observations to survey customers' profile and their perceptions with regard to the concept of smart grids.

The research included the an random sample of more than 70 residents of Fernando de Noronha Island, and the results guided technical decisions in the remainder of the project.

  • Client: CELPE

Iconic Authentication

User Research

I conducted interviews with users in order to understand the authentication process in smartphones, and identify improvements points.

We evaluated prototypes of iconic authentication, considering different icons repertoire, password size, among other parameters. In addition to conventional metrics, we analyze eye tracking data from user interaction.

  • Client: CPqD BIOMODAL Project

Usability and Eye Tracking Evaluation

Interface Evaluation

We conducted usability tests complemented with eye tracking data of the Virtual Agency of CEMIG. 10 participants were randomly invited to explorer and perform 4 quick tasks at CEMIG Virtual Agency.

The results were analyzed qualitatively, using strong and weak points indicated by participants, difficult level, satisfaction and participants' performance in the tasks. We delivered a report with improvement recommendations for CEMIG.

  • Client: CEMIG Virtual Agency

Accessibility Evaluation

Interface Evaluation

As the law enforces that banks must provice services accessible for all, I participated in the team to evaluate the accessibility of Internet Banking of Mercantil do Brasil.

Beyonde evaluate the adequacy of the code to web accessibility standars, such as WCAG and eMAG, we invited a blind user to explore and perform tasks using the industry leading screen readers.

We use think-aloud protocol and the problems found were categorized and used as input in the system redesign.

  • Client: Mercantil do Brasil Internet Banking
